  1. Visiting the Nairobi national Park in Kenya is an experience like no other.
  2. Just a short drive from the city center you get to enjoy the sight of majestice animals such as the Big five roaming freely against the back drop of Nairobi.
  3. Despite being close to the urban area the park is home to a wide variety of wildlife such as lions, giraffes, rhinos, Zebras and over 400 bird species.
  4. Its one of the rare national parks world wide located in the middle of a capital city.
